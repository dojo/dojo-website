dijit/_OnDijitClickMixin.a11yclick (version 1.9)

Custom press, release, and click synthetic events which trigger on a left mouse click, touch, or space/enter keyup.

Method Summary

  • click(node,listener) Logical click operation for mouse, touch, or keyboard (space/enter key)
  • move(node,listener) Mouse cursor or a finger is dragged over the given node.
  • press(node,listener) Mousedown (left button), touchstart, or keydown (space or enter) corresponding to logical click operation.
  • release(node,listener) Mouseup (left button), touchend, or keyup (space or enter) corresponding to logical click operation.
